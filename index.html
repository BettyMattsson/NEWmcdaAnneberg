Hierarchical Weighting Survey for Anneberg

📋 Introduction and background

This survey is part of a master's thesis at Chalmers University of Technology. The results will help determine which stormwater solutions are most suitable for the study area. The area consists of two schools and apartment buildings. The solutions are named "referense alternative" and ALT 1-4 to ensure objectivity.

📝 Your task

Distribute 100 points between the criteria in each step, based on your personal experience and priorities, as well as the number lines.

More points = more importance

📊 The Number lines

To aid in the weigting of the criteria, we have scored the solutions on a scale from -10 to +10 (visualized on number lines for each criterion) based on expertise. We use a reference alternative as a baseline, which corresponds to 0 on the scale.

In brief, this means:

  • Plus points: Better than the reference alternative.
  • 0 points: Equivalent to the reference alternative.
  • Minus points: Worse than the reference alternative.

You might give more points to a criterion that you perceive as important and where the number line shows large differences between solutions. Conversely, a criterion that you also perceive as important might receive fewer points if all solutions perform similarly.

The number lines help you see where the differences are — your own priorities always come first.
